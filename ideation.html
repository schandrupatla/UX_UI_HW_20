Information Architecture

Based on the insights gained from the initial content audits, competitor analyzes and Card Sorts with potential users, we defined the sitemap for ADOPT A STAR and then evaluated it via tree tests with potential users.

As a team we contacted “Travis Metcalfe”, an professional astronomer at the Space Science Institute in Boulder, Colorado who started this nonprofit “Adopt a star”. He provided us with the insight data. We also conducted user test and gathered user test insights which helped us to create “Empathy”.
